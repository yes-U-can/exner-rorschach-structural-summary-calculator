# Document Name: [Interpretation/Affect] Blends:R

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Blends:R
- complexity index

## Core Definition

`Blends:R` expresses the proportion of responses that contain two or more determinants relative to total responses.
In affect, it helps estimate how much complexity and mixture of emotional states appears in experience.

## Interpretation Points

- If the ratio is high, there may be greater affective complexity and more emotional layers active at the same time.
- If it is low, experience may appear simpler, less nuanced, or less integrated.
- Both the ratio and total `R` should be checked, because a highly productive protocol can lower the ratio.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- A high ratio does not automatically mean healthy flexibility; it can also accompany tension or overload.
- A low ratio does not necessarily imply emotional poverty, especially when `R` is small or `Lambda` is high.
- Color-shading blends deserve added attention because they can signal mixed affect and distress.
