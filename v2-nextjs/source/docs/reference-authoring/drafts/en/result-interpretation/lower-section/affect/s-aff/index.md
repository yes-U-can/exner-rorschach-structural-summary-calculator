# Document Name: [Interpretation/Affect] S

## Aliases / Search Terms

- S
- white space affect

## Core Definition

`S` in affect refers to the frequency of responses that use white space with emotional interpretive value.
It is reviewed as a possible sign of opposition, self-assertion, tension, or distance from the stimulus field.

## Interpretation Points

- If `S` is high, there may be more tendency to respond from opposition, negativism, or defensive self-assertion.
- In some protocols it can also reflect anger, internal pressure, or a need to mark distance.
- Not every white-space response is hostility; some cases reflect exploratory style or perceptual searching.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- It should not be confused with `S` in the upper section or with `S-%` in mediation.
- A small number of cases is not enough to define a stable oppositional style.
- It is useful to see whether white-space use appears together with `AG`, `CP`, or broader tension markers.
