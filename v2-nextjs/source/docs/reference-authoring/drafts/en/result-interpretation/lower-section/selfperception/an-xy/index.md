# Document Name: [Interpretation/Self Perception] An + Xy
## Aliases / Search Terms
- An+Xy
- anatomy plus x-ray
- body concern

## Core Definition
`An+Xy` combines anatomy content and x-ray content.
In `Self Perception`, it functions as a clue to concern about the body, its functioning, or its vulnerability.
## Interpretation Points
- If the total is high, there may be greater focus on organs, bodily damage, illness, or physical fragility.
- The variable can appear in somatic concern, body anxiety, or recent health-related stress.
- If it is combined with `MOR_self`, body image may feel more damaged, weak, or alarming.
- Its meaning depends on whether the body appears realistic, feared, or symbolically loaded.
## Cautions / Distinctions
- Medical training, real illness, or recent treatment can raise this variable without special psychopathological meaning.
- `Xy` does not always carry exactly the same meaning as `An` in each response, so the response context still matters.
- A low value does not rule out all body concern.
## Limits of Isolated Interpretation
- The variable points to bodily focus, but does not decide by itself whether that focus is hypochondriacal, traumatic, or situational.
- It should be read together with `MOR_self`, `SumV_self`, and `HVI`.
## Variables to Review Together

- `An`
- `Xy`
- `HVI`
- `SumV_self`
