# Document Name: [Interpretation/Mediation] X-%

## Aliases / Search Terms

- X-%
- Distorted Form

## Core Definition

`X-%` indicates the proportion of responses with clearly distorted form use.
In mediation, it functions as a central sign of perceptual distance from shared reality.

## Interpretation Points

- If `X-%` rises, the frequency of perceptions that do not fit the blot well increases.
- If `Xu%` is also high, there may be a mixture of tolerable oddness and genuine distortion.
- If `X-%` is high together with `WSum6` or `PTI`, clinical concern increases.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- `X-%` is not the same as `Xu%`; `Xu%` still reflects an uncommon but possible reading.
- It should be reviewed together with total responses and the broader distribution of `FQ-`.
- A high value is informative, but it does not by itself explain the source of distortion.
