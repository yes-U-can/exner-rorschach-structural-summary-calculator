# Document Name: [Interpretation/Core] Lambda

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Lambda
- L
- lambda

## Core Definition

`Lambda` is the ratio between pure-form responses and the rest of the responses.
In interpretation it is used as an indicator of how much the protocol simplifies, economizes, or restricts processing.

## Interpretation Points

- High `Lambda` often marks a simpler, more economical, or more defensive style.
- In protocols of sufficient length, high `Lambda` may accompany avoidance, field reduction, or simplified handling of the stimulus.
- Very low `Lambda` may be associated with greater complexity, but also with overinclusion or excessive openness.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- If `R` is low, high `Lambda` may reflect guardedness more than a stable style.
- Low `Lambda` is not automatically a strength; if resources are weak, it may coexist with inefficiency.
- `Zd`, `W:D:Dd`, `EB`, `EA`, `D`, and `AdjD` should be reviewed with it.
