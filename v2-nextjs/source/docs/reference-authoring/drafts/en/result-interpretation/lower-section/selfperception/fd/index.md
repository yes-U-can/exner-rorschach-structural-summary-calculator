# Document Name: [Interpretation/Self Perception] FD
## Aliases / Search Terms
- FD
- form dimension
- introspection

## Core Definition
`FD` shows a tendency to step back from experience and to observe the self.
In `Self Perception`, it is used as a clue to introspection and conscious self-observation.
## Interpretation Points
- If `FD > 0`, there is usually some degree of inward observation of personal functioning.
- That observation can be adaptive, or it can take on criticism, guilt, or discouragement.
- If `FD` appears together with `SumV` or `MOR_self`, introspection may feel heavier or more painful.
- If `FD` appears with a high egocentricity index, much attention may be fixed on the self.
## Cautions / Distinctions
- A high `FD` does not automatically equal good insight.
- `FD = 0` does not prove the total absence of introspection.
- It should be distinguished from the determinant code `FD` and from the content code `Fd`.
## Limits of Isolated Interpretation
- `FD` shows the presence of self-observation, not its quality by itself.
- It should be read together with `SumV`, `MOR_self`, and `3r+(2)/R`.
