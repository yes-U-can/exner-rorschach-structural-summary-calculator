# Document Name: [Interpretation/Ideation] Ma:Mp

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Ma:Mp
- active:passive human movement
- human movement active:passive

## Core Definition

`Ma:Mp` expresses the ratio between active and passive human movement within `M` responses.
It helps show whether imagined human activity tends to be represented as acting and intervening or as waiting, receiving, or being acted upon.

## Interpretation Points

- If `Mp` exceeds `Ma`, human ideation may more often represent passivity, waiting, or receptivity.
- If `Ma` predominates, human ideation may lean more toward initiative, intervention, or directing action.
- When the ratio is balanced, human representations are not strongly pulled toward one pole.
- Its meaning becomes more useful when integrated with the quality of `M`.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- If the number of `M` responses is very low, the ratio loses stability.
- Predominant `Mp` does not by itself imply fragility, and predominant `Ma` does not by itself imply positive adjustment.
- The quality of ideation still has to be checked through `M-` and `Mnone`.
