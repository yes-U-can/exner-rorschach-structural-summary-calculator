# Document Name: [Interpretation/Lower Section/Ideation] Ideation

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Ideation
- ideation
- thinking

## Core Definition

`Ideation` is the group of variables used to read the direction of thought, the organization of representations, and the presence of cognitive peculiarities or slippage.
In this section, `a:p`, `Ma:Mp`, `2AB+Art+Ay`, `MOR`, `Sum6`, `Lv2`, `WSum6`, `M-`, and `Mnone` are reviewed together.

## Interpretation Points

- `a:p` and `Ma:Mp` help show whether mental activity leans more toward initiative or passivity.
- `2AB+Art+Ay` shows how much thought shifts toward abstract, artificial, or less immediate formulations.
- `MOR` adds the weight of damage, failure, deterioration, or loss within ideational content.
- `Sum6`, `Lv2`, and `WSum6` summarize the magnitude of cognitive peculiarities.
- `M-` and `Mnone` help show how well thought linked to human movement is organized.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- This group should not be read without considering protocol quality and the context of `Mediation` and `Processing`.
- A mildly elevated single variable is not enough to conclude major ideational distortion.
- When `M` is very scarce, some ratios or contrasts lose stability.
