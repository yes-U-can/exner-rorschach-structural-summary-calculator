# Document Name: [Interpretation/Ideation] M-

## Aliases / Search Terms

- M-
- minus human movement
- poor form human movement

## Core Definition

`M-` indicates human movement responses with minus form quality.
In interpretation, it is used as a sign that imagined human activity is distorted, poorly anchored in shared reality, or less accurately organized.

## Interpretation Points

- If `M-` appears, human representations may show less precise or less reality-based thinking.
- If it accumulates with `Lv2` or `WSum6`, concern about ideational disorganization increases.
- It can point to odd human fantasy, distorted reading of other people, or less integrated mental elaboration.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- Not every unusual `M` response is `M-`; the `M` coding and form quality both have to be confirmed first.
- A single `M-` is not enough to define a thought disorder.
- Its weight changes greatly depending on how it combines with `Mnone`, `PTI`, mediation, and processing.
