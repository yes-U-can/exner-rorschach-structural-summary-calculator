# Document Name: [Interpretation/Core] FM

## Aliases / Search Terms

- FM
- animal movement total
- total animal movement

## Core Definition

`FM` is the total number of animal movement responses.
In `core`, it usually acts as a component on the left side of `eb`, reflecting need tension, pressure for discharge, and motivational demand that is not yet fully elaborated.

## Interpretation Points

- Moderate `FM` can suggest that needs and inner pressures are present and pushing behavior.
- High `FM` can suggest greater pressure from unsatisfied needs, drives, or discharge demands.
- `FM` contributes to `eb` and, together with `m`, can raise `es`.
- Its meaning becomes clearer when compared with `m`, `D`, and `AdjD`.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- High `FM` does not by itself prove openly disorganized impulse control.
- `FM` should not be confused with `M`; here it points to more basic or less integrated pressure rather than mature ideational elaboration.
- Its clinical weight changes according to the balance with `m`, `es`, `D`, and `AdjD`.
