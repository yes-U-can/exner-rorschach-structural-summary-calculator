# Document Name: [Interpretation/Core] R

## Aliases / Search Terms

- R
- response total
- total number of responses

## Core Definition

`R` is the total number of responses in the protocol.
In interpretation it functions first as an indicator of range, productivity, and record adequacy.

## Interpretation Points

- Very low `R` requires great caution in reading the protocol.
- A sufficient `R` gives a more stable basis for interpreting the rest of the structural summary.
- High `R` is not automatically a strength; it may also accompany expansiveness or overproduction.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- Low `R` may reflect defensiveness, withdrawal, fatigue, situational anxiety, or problems in the test relationship.
- High `R` does not by itself imply adaptive richness.
- The meaning of `R` changes with `Lambda`, `DQ`, and test behavior.
