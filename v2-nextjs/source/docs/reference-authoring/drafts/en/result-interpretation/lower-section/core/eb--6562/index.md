# Document Name: [Interpretation/Core] eb

## Aliases / Search Terms

- eb
- Experience Base
- experienced-stimulation base

## Core Definition

`eb` is the ratio `FM + m : SumShd`.
It describes the composition of current demand: whether the burden is weighted more by unmet-needs tension and active unrest, or more by diffuse discomfort, shading, and dysphoric affect.

## Interpretation Points

- If `FM + m` predominates, current demand may lean toward active tension, unrest, or pressure around impulse and need.
- If `SumShd` predominates, diffuse discomfort, affective strain, or internal uneasiness may weigh more heavily.
- `eb` describes the composition of demand; it is not the same as the total amount of burden.
- It prepares the way for reading `es`, `D`, `AdjEs`, and `AdjD`.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- `eb` is a ratio, not a total magnitude.
- It is important to review the specific components that create it: `FM`, `m`, `C'`, `T`, `V`, and `Y`.
- In short protocols, the ratio may look exaggerated or unstable.
