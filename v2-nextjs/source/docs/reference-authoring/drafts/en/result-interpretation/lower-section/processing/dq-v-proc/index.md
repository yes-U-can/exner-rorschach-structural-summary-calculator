# Document Name: [Interpretation/Processing] DQv

## Aliases / Search Terms

- DQv
- Vague Developmental Quality
- processing DQv

## Core Definition

`DQv` indicates how much vague, simplified, or weakly integrated organization appears in processing.
In interpretation, it works as a sign that organizational quality may drift toward less elaborated forms.

## Interpretation Points

- If `DQv` rises, processing may become simpler, less integrated, or less precise.
- When `DQv` and `DQv/+` outweigh `DQ+`, processing quality tends to look more fragile.
- The value gains meaning when compared with `Zd`, `PSV`, and the overall developmental quality pattern.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- `DQv` should be read together with `DQv/+`, not treated as the same thing.
- A high value does not by itself explain why organization is reduced.
- It should not be confused with automatic clinical severity.

## Limits of Isolated Interpretation

- `DQv` suggests a weak point in processing, but it does not explain the entire cognitive style by itself.
- It should be read with `DQ+`, `PSV`, `X-%`, and `WSum6`.

## Variables to Review Together

- `DQ+`
- `DQv/+`
- `PSV`
- `X-%`
- `WSum6`
