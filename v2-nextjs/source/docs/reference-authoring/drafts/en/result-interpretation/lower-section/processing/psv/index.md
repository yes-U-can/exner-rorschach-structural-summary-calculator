# Document Name: [Interpretation/Processing] PSV

## Aliases / Search Terms

- PSV
- Perseveration
- processing perseveration

## Core Definition

`PSV` indicates a tendency to repeat the same approach or remain fixed on a prior pattern during processing.
In interpretation, it functions as a sign of rigidity, difficulty shifting focus, or inefficient attentional control.

## Interpretation Points

- When `PSV` is present, it is worth checking whether the record shows less flexibility in changing strategy.
- `PSV` combined with other inefficiency indicators strengthens the reading of rigid processing.
- In some records, even a single `PSV` deserves attention because the variable is meant to capture genuine perseveration.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- `PSV` is not the same as normal consistency or stylistic preference.
- The value needs protocol context and the exact form of the coded perseveration.
- It should not be overread without reviewing `Zd`, `DQ`, and other signs of organization.

## Limits of Isolated Interpretation

- `PSV` is a warning sign, not a diagnosis by itself.
- It should be read with `Zd`, `DQ+`, `DQv`, `WSum6`, and `X-%`.

## Variables to Review Together

- `Zd`
- `DQ+`
- `DQv`
- `WSum6`
- `X-%`
