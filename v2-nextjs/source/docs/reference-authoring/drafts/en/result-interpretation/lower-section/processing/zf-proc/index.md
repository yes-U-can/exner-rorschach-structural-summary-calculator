# Document Name: [Interpretation/Processing] Zf

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Zf
- processing Zf
- Z frequency

## Core Definition

`Zf` indicates how often the person actively tries to scan and organize the field during processing.
In this section it is used as a frequency measure of organizational effort, not as a measure of efficiency by itself.

## Interpretation Points

- If `Zf` is high, there are usually more attempts to examine and organize the stimulus.
- If `Zf` is low, there may be less active exploration or less initiative to structure the field.
- Its clinical meaning changes when read together with `Zd`, because frequency and efficiency are not the same.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- A high `Zf` does not automatically mean good processing.
- A low `Zf` can be expected if the protocol is short.
- To interpret the value, the `W:D:Dd` distribution should also be reviewed.
