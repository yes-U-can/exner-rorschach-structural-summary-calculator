# Document Name: [Interpretation/Self Perception] Fr + rF

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Fr+rF
- reflection responses
- self-reflection responses

## Core Definition

`Fr+rF` shows how many reflection responses appear in the protocol.
In `Self Perception`, it helps read whether the self-image needs special shine, confirmation, or an inflated sense of personal importance.

## Interpretation Points

- If `Fr+rF > 0`, it is worth reviewing increased self-importance or the need to reinforce self-image.
- The variable may relate more to need for reaffirmation than to calm confidence.
- If the egocentricity index is also elevated, self-focus gains even more weight.
- If it appears together with `SumV`, there may be an unstable mix of self-enhancement and self-criticism.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- In adults, reflection responses are not common, so even one instance deserves review.
- Even so, a single reflection does not define the entire personality style.
- Its absence does not prove there is no narcissistic sensitivity or need for validation.

## Limits of Isolated Interpretation

- It should be integrated with `3r+(2)/R`, `SumV`, `FD`, and `MOR`.
- The variable indicates one direction of self-image, not its total stability.

## Variables to Review Together

- `3r+(2)/R`
- `SumV_self`
- `FD`
- `Fr`
- `rF`
