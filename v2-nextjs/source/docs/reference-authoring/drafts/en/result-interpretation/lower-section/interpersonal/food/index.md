# Document Name: [Interpretation/Interpersonal] Food

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Food
- food content
- dependency imagery

## Core Definition

`Food` shows how much food content appears when it is read on the interpersonal level.
In interpretation, it is used as a secondary indicator of need for care, support, or dependency in relatedness.

## Interpretation Points

- In adults, the presence of `Food` usually invites review of needs for support or relational gratification.
- If it appears together with `SumT_inter`, the search for closeness may look stronger.
- The variable is not mainly about literal hunger; it is more often used as a symbolic clue to needing to be fed or cared for.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- In child protocols, its frequency may carry different weight than it does in adults.
- `Food = 1` is not enough to conclude intense dependency.
- Coding `Fd` should not be confused with automatic interpretation of interpersonal deprivation.

## Limits of Isolated Interpretation

- `Food` is a supplementary variable.
- It should be read with `SumT_inter`, `COP`, `ISO_Index`, and `CDI`.
