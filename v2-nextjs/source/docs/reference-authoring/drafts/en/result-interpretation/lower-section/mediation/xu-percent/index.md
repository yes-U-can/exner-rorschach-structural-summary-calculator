# Document Name: [Interpretation/Mediation] Xu%

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Xu%
- Unusual Form

## Core Definition

`Xu%` indicates the proportion of responses that are formally acceptable but less usual or less conventional.
It marks a style of mediation that can still be sustained, even though it departs further from consensus.

## Interpretation Points

- If `Xu%` is high, there may be more personal or less conventional translations of the blot.
- If it rises while `X-%` stays low, the oddness may remain tolerable rather than clearly distorted.
- If `Xu%` and `X-%` rise together, perception may become less dependable.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- `Xu%` is not the same as `X-%`; `Xu%` remains acceptable, while `X-%` indicates stronger distortion.
- A high `Xu%` should not be confused immediately with pathology.
- Its meaning changes depending on its relation to `X+%`, `XA%`, `P`, and the rest of the protocol.
