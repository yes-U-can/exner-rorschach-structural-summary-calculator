# Document Name: [Interpretation/Interpersonal] Pure H

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Pure H
- pure human
- whole human content

## Core Definition

`PureH` shows how much human content appears in complete and conventional form, that is, as `H`.
At the interpersonal level, it helps show whether the image of the other tends to be organized as a whole person rather than as a part-object, partial figure, or fantasy human.

## Interpretation Points

- If `PureH` is relatively high, there is usually more availability to represent people as whole and more integrated figures.
- If `PureH` is low, especially when `HumanCont` is not low, it is worth checking whether human representations are more partial, idealized, or distorted.
- `PureH < 2` often justifies a more careful review of relational style and the quality of human content.
- The variable reflects structural quality of human representation, not interpersonal kindness or sympathy.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- `PureH` is not the same as `HumanCont`; one looks at complete human content and the other looks at total human content.
- A high value does not guarantee mature relatedness if `PHR`, `AG`, or `MOR` are also high.
- A low value does not by itself mean rejection of people.

## Limits of Isolated Interpretation

- To read `PureH` well, it should be compared with `HumanCont`, `GHR`, `PHR`, and `H ratio`.
- Its meaning changes if the protocol contains almost no human responses.
