# Document Name: [Interpretation/Processing] DQ+

## Aliases / Search Terms

- DQ+
- Developmental Quality Plus
- processing DQ+

## Core Definition

`DQ+` indicates how much integrated, sophisticated organization appears in processing.
In interpretation, it is used as a sign of capacity to structure percepts in a complex and articulated way.

## Interpretation Points

- If `DQ+` is adequately present, there is usually better capacity to organize complex material in an integrated form.
- When `DQ+` is sustained and `DQv` does not weigh too heavily, processing may look more elaborated and sophisticated.
- The value gains meaning when compared with `Zd`, `PSV`, and the general distribution of developmental quality.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- A high `DQ+` does not automatically mean excellent adaptation.
- If `DQ+` is high but `DQv` is also high, organization may still be uneven.
- `DQ+` should not be confused with `v/+`; they belong to the same coding family but serve different interpretive roles.

## Limits of Isolated Interpretation

- `DQ+` shows a sign of processing sophistication, not a total summary of functioning.
- It should be read with `DQv`, `Zd`, `PSV`, and `W:M`.
