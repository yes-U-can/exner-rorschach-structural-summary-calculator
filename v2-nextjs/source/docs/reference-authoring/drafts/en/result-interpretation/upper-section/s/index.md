# Document Name: [Interpretation/Upper Section] S

## Aliases / Search Terms

- S
- space responses
- white space responses

## Core Definition

`S` is the frequency of responses that use white space as the main location.
Within `Upper Section` it is read as a clue about reversed focus, opposition, or unconventional exploration of the field.

## Interpretation Points

- Some `S` may accompany independent focus or critical exploration of the stimulus.
- High `S` may suggest opposition, nonconformity, or a tendency to look where others do not.
- Low `S` simply indicates that white space was not an important resource in the protocol.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- High `S` should not be reduced immediately to rebellion.
- If `S` rises with `S-%`, `X-%`, `AG`, or `PER`, the meaning may lean toward greater conflict or inefficiency.
- If it appears with good mediation and good formal control, it may also reflect flexibility or originality.
