# Document Name: [Interpretation/Upper Section] DQ+

## Aliases / Search Terms

- DQ+
- synthesized responses
- number of DQ+ responses

## Core Definition

`DQ+` is the frequency of responses with well-integrated developmental quality.
In interpretation it indicates how often complex and well-articulated organization appears in the protocol.

## Interpretation Points

- The presence of `DQ+` suggests capacity to integrate parts of the stimulus into more complex configurations.
- Relatively high `DQ+` may reflect greater organizational complexity available within the protocol.
- Low `DQ+` may indicate predominance of simpler responses or fewer attempts at integration.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- High `DQ+` should not automatically be interpreted as superior cognitive ability.
- If `DQ+` is high but `FQ` or `Zd` are weak, the complexity may not be efficient.
- Low `DQ+` can also appear in brief or highly defensive protocols.
