# Document Name: [Interpretation/Upper Section] Dd

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Dd
- unusual detail
- rare detail responses

## Core Definition

`Dd` is the frequency of responses given to unusual or infrequently used details.
In interpretation it indicates how much attention shifts toward less shared or less obvious parts of the stimulus.

## Interpretation Points

- Some `Dd` may suggest exploratory breadth or sensitivity to less salient detail.
- High `Dd` may accompany idiosyncratic focus, unconventional interest, or very peripheral scanning.
- Very low `Dd` may reflect staying with more typical and expected units.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- High `Dd` does not automatically equal creativity.
- If `Dd` rises with low `P` and high `Xu%`, the reading leans more toward unconventional perception.
- If `Dd` rises with very positive `Zd`, overinspection may be more likely than creativity.
