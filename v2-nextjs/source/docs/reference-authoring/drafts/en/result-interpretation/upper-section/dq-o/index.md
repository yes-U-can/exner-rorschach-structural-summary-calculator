# Document Name: [Interpretation/Upper Section] DQo

## Aliases / Search Terms

- DQo
- ordinary developmental quality
- number of DQo responses

## Core Definition

`DQo` is the frequency of responses with ordinary developmental quality.
It usually functions as the baseline for the protocol's usual level of organization.

## Interpretation Points

- In many protocols, `DQo` forms the central part of the `DQ` distribution.
- A proportionate `DQo` may reflect a common, stable level of organization.
- A very strong predominance of `DQo` may indicate a fairly conventional and not very complex style.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- High `DQo` is not necessarily a weakness.
- Its meaning changes with the relation to `DQ+`, `DQv/+`, and `DQv`.
- If `DQo` dominates and `DQ+` is almost absent, integrative complexity may be limited.
