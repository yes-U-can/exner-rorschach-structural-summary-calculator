# Document Name: [Coding/Cards] III

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Card III
- card III
- card 3
- plate III

## Core Definition

`Card III` often facilitates human responses and scenes of interaction.  
This page functions as a coding reminder for human content, movement, and relational quality.

## Application Conditions

- Responses with two people, two human figures, or figures in relation are common.
- If human movement is clear, review `M`.
- If the response shows cooperation or aggression between moving figures, review `COP` and `AG`.
- Because human content often appears here, it is also useful to review `H` and the `GHR / PHR` decision.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- The mere presence of two figures is not enough for `COP` or `AG`.
- A response that looks social does not automatically mean human movement.
- If the scene is organized as two separate objects without a defined action, do not add relational codes just by intuition.
