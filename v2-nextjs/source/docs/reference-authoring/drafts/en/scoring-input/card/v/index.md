# Document Name: [Coding/Cards] V

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Card V
- card V
- card 5
- plate V

## Core Definition

`Card V` often facilitates relatively clear form responses and global popular responses.  
This page summarizes coding points that are especially useful on that plate.

## Application Conditions

- `W` responses are common.
- The relatively clear form of the blot makes it useful to review `F` and `FQ`.
- Many popular responses on this card include bat or butterfly.
- If the response uses the whole blot with good organization, review `Popular` together with the formal decision.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- `Card V` is not the same as determinant `V` for Vista.
- Although `Card I` and `Card V` share some typical responses, they should not be merged as if they were the same plate.
- Do not add a determinant just because the person says the blot looks dark or symmetrical.
