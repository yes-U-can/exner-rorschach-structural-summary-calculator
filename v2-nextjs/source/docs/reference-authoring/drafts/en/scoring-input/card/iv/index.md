# Document Name: [Coding/Cards] IV

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Card IV
- card IV
- card 4
- plate IV

## Core Definition

`Card IV` often facilitates global responses and comments tied to form, texture, or visual weight.  
This page summarizes what is useful to review when coding those responses.

## Application Conditions

- `W` responses are common on `Card IV`.
- If the subject describes texture or tactile quality, review `T` or `TF` depending on the role of form.
- If the response remains mainly formal, review `F` and `FQ`.
- The fact that the card looks heavy or shaded does not by itself add a shading determinant.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- Verbal descriptions of roughness or weight are not enough unless they actually shape the coded perception.
- This page does not interpret the symbolism of the card; it only flags coding focal points.
- `Card IV` and determinant `V` are different things and should not be confused.
