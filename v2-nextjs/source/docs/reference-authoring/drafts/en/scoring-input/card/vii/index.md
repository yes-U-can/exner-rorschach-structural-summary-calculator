# Document Name: [Coding/Cards] VII

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Card VII
- card VII
- card 7
- plate VII

## Core Definition

`Card VII` often facilitates heads, faces, and partial human figures.  
This page summarizes what to review in human content, organization, and movement.

## Application Conditions

- Face, head, or other partial human responses are common.
- If the response is partial, review `Hd` before `H`.
- If the scene shows clear human action, review `M`.
- When the organization among figures or zones becomes important, it may also be useful to review `Z`.
- Human content may also lead you to review `GHR / PHR`.

## Cautions / Distinctions

- A response that looks human does not automatically mean full human content `H`.
- Two profiles or two faces do not by themselves create a relational code.
- This page remains a coding note, not a symbolic interpretation of the card.
