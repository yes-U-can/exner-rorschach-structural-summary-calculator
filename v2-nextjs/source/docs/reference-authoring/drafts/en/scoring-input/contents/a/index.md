# Document Name: [Coding/Content] A

## Aliases / Search Terms

- A
- whole animal
- real animal

## Core Definition

`A` is coded when the response content is a whole, realistic animal.  
It does not refer to an animal part or a fantastic creature.

## Application Conditions

- The seen figure must organize as a whole animal.
- The referent has to be recognizable as a real animal, not an imagined one.
- Typical examples are a whole dog, bat, butterfly, or bird.
- If only part of the animal appears, first consider [`Ad`](ref://scoring-input/contents/Ad).
- If the whole animal is fantastic, mythic, or invented, first consider [`(A)`](ref://scoring-input/contents/%28A%29).

## Cautions / Distinctions

- `A` and [`(A)`](ref://scoring-input/contents/%28A%29) share the idea of a whole animal, but differ in whether the referent is real or imagined.
- `A` and [`Ad`](ref://scoring-input/contents/Ad) are separated by wholeness, not by size.
- `A` content by itself does not decide `Popular`, `FQ`, or the determinant.
