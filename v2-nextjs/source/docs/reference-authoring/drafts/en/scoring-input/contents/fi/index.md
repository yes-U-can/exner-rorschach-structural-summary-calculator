# Document Name: [Coding/Content] Fi

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Fi
- fire
- smoke

## Core Definition

`Fi` is coded when the main content is fire or smoke.  
The response has to organize the blot as flame, fire, smoke, or a column of smoke.

## Application Conditions

- Use it for flames, fire, burning, smoke, or a smoke column when they are clearly named.
- The focus should stay on fire or smoke themselves.
- If the response emphasizes explosion or detonation, first reconsider [`Ex`](ref://scoring-input/contents/Ex).
- If the person is describing clouds, first reconsider [`Cl`](ref://scoring-input/contents/Cl).

## Cautions / Distinctions

- `Fi` and [`Ex`](ref://scoring-input/contents/Ex) are not separated by visual intensity; they are separated by the named content.
- `Fi` and [`Cl`](ref://scoring-input/contents/Cl) can look alike when the form is diffuse, but one is smoke/fire and the other is cloud.
- Do not assign `Fi` just because of red or dark color; the person has to say fire or smoke.
