# Document Name: [Coding/Content] Cg

## Aliases / Search Terms

- Cg
- clothing
- garment
- wearable

## Core Definition

`Cg` is coded when the main content is clothing, a garment, or a wearable accessory.  
The decisive point is that the form is understood as something worn on the body.

## Application Conditions

- Use it for hat, boot, glove, cape, dress, uniform, or another recognizable item of clothing.
- It may also be used for body-worn accessories when the response treats them as part of dress.
- If the garment carries a dominant historical or cultural reference, it may also require [`Ay`](ref://scoring-input/contents/Ay).
- If the emphasis falls on decorative or artistic value, it may also require [`Art`](ref://scoring-input/contents/Art).

## Cautions / Distinctions

- `Cg` is not used for just any object. The response has to identify clothing or a wearable accessory.
- `Cg` and [`Ay`](ref://scoring-input/contents/Ay) may coexist, but `Ay` needs cultural or historical framing.
- `Cg` and [`Art`](ref://scoring-input/contents/Art) may coexist, but `Art` needs the object to be understood as artistic or decorative.
