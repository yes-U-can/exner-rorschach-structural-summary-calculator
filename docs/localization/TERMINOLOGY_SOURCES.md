# Terminology Sources

Status: Reviewed evidence register

This register records where recurring public-document terms were checked. It is a starting point for human or independent editorial review, not a substitute for the Exner Comprehensive System manuals or professional judgment.

## Source Priority

1. Official manuals, authorized translations, and national or international Rorschach professional associations
2. Peer-reviewed target-language clinical research and academic publishers
3. University repositories and professional training material
4. Existing project corpus and prior translations, used only to find terms that still require external confirmation

A search snippet, machine translation, commercial software page, or third-party document copy may identify a lead but does not by itself approve terminology.

## Korean

- [Exner의 종합체계에 근거한 한국형 아동 로르샤하 종합체계의 구성: 탐색적 연구](https://www.kci.go.kr/kciportal/landing/article.kci?arti_id=ART001392063), Korean Journal of Clinical Psychology, 2009. Confirms Korean professional use of `Exner`, `종합체계`, Rorschach variables, form quality, and coding context.
- [Rorschach 검사 규준에 관한 동향 연구](https://kiss.kstudy.com/DetailOa/Ar?key=52242922), 재활심리연구, 2021. Helps distinguish the Comprehensive System from later systems and international normative work.

Project naming intentionally retains `로샤` and `구조요약`. Academic sources may use `로르샤하` or `구조적 요약`; those variants must not silently rename the product.

## English

- [An examination of interrater reliability for scoring the Rorschach Comprehensive System in eight data sets](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/12067192/), Journal of Personality Assessment, 2002. Confirms `Comprehensive System`, `scoring`, and `summary scores` in peer-reviewed use.
- [Interobserver Agreement, Intraobserver Reliability, and the Rorschach Comprehensive System](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1207/S15327752JPA740103), Journal of Personality Assessment, 2000. Confirms `Structural Summary`, response-level codes, ratios, percentages, and derivations.
- [Coding and Interpreting Movement on the Rorschach](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1177/107319119600300201), Assessment, 3(2), 1996. Confirms `human movement`, `animal movement`, `inanimate movement`, and the Active/Passive quality of movement.
- Exner, J. E. (2003). *The Rorschach: A Comprehensive System, Volume 1* (4th ed.), Wiley. Primary manual for `Comprehensive System`, `Structural Summary`, `determinant`, the Response and `Inquiry` phases, and active/passive movement quality.

## Japanese

- [包括システムによるロールシャッハ臨床](https://www.seishinshobo.co.jp/book/b88274.html), 誠信書房. Its contents confirm `包括システム`, `構造一覧表`, `スコアリング（記号化）`, `自由反応段階`, `質問段階`, and `決定因` in Japanese professional publishing.
- [包括システムの構造一覧表に関する最近の改訂についての解説](https://jglobal.jst.go.jp/en/detail?JGLOBAL_ID=201102161463734325), 包括システムによる日本ロールシャッハ学会誌, 2001. Confirms `構造一覧表` in the Japanese Comprehensive System literature.
- [包括システムによるロールシャッハ・テスト認定資格](https://www.jrscweb.com/_src/57075845/%E3%83%AC%E3%83%99%E3%83%AB1%EF%BC%88%E5%9F%BA%E7%A4%8E%EF%BC%8FCPCS-1%EF%BC%89%E3%81%AB%E4%BF%82%E3%82%8B%E7%B4%B0%E5%89%87.pdf?v=1746625959175), 包括システムによる日本ロールシャッハ学会. Confirms contemporary professional use of `コーディング` and `構造一覧表`.
- [ロールシャッハ包括システム 構造一覧表記録用紙](https://www.kongoshuppan.co.jp/book/b515113.html), 金剛出版. Official Comprehensive System record form; confirms `構造一覧表` as the standard published label for the Structural Summary. Cross-checked against the 日本ロールシャッハ学会 record-form listing ("ロールシャッハ包括システム構造一覧表記録用紙").
- ジョン・E・エクスナー 著／中村紀子・野田昌道 訳 [ロールシャッハ・テスト―包括システムの基礎と解釈の原理](https://www.kongoshuppan.co.jp/book/b514556.html), 金剛出版. Authorized Japanese translation of the CS manual (publisher/translator-level authority) for `包括システム`, `構造一覧表`, `決定因`, `質問段階`.
- [ロールシャッハ運動反応におけるactive-passiveの概念と精神病理との関係](http://hdl.handle.net/10291/15733), 明治大学心理社会学研究, 2013. Uses `活動性概念（active-passive）` and `運動の活動性` in Japanese academic discussion of active/passive movement coding.

`構造一覧表` is confirmed by the national society and the authorized translation. For active/passive movement coding, the glossary uses `運動の活動性（能動・受動）`, following the peer-reviewed Japanese wording above. Natural prose describing a missing marker uses `能動・受動の区別がない`; the unsupported literal phrases `能動・受動の質` and `能動・受動の性質` are not used as fixed terms.

## Spanish

- [CHESSSS](https://rorschachspain.org/chessss/), Sociedad Española de Rorschach y Métodos Proyectivos. Confirms `Sumario Estructural` and `Sistema Comprehensivo` in Spanish professional use.
- [Formación de FERSiC](https://fersic.org.ar/index.php?Itemid=229&id=42&option=com_content&view=article), Fundación Estudios de Rorschach Sistema Comprehensivo. Confirms `administrar`, `codificar`, `protocolo`, and `sumario estructural` in Spanish clinical training.
- [El lenguaje Rorschach y el sistema comprehensivo](https://dialnet.unirioja.es/descarga/articulo/5205706.pdf), Subjetividad y Procesos Cognitivos, 2015. Confirms `codificación`, `calidad evolutiva`, `determinantes`, `calidad formal`, and `sumario estructural` in academic prose.
- Exner, J. E. *Manual de Codificación del Rorschach para el Sistema Comprehensivo* (3.ª ed.), Psimática, Madrid (ISBN 9788488909244); and M. C. Sendín, *Manual de interpretación del Rorschach para el Sistema Comprehensivo*, Psimática. Authorized Spanish translations of Exner's manuals; confirm `Sistema Comprehensivo`, `Sumario Estructural`, `codificación`, and `encuesta` for the Inquiry phase.

`encuesta` for the Inquiry phase is confirmed by the authorized Psimática translations of Exner's Comprehensive System manuals. The coding-manual section title `Los superíndices activo-pasivo` supports `superíndice activo o pasivo` for the recurring coding label; companion prose uses that wording rather than treating active/passive marking as a general `cualidad`.

## Brazilian Portuguese

- [Indícios de validade do déficit relacional no Método de Rorschach para população adulta não-paciente](https://www.scielo.br/j/pusf/a/6Xy8zSJGCNq49BWjXRpYNhx/?lang=pt), Psico-USF, 2012. Confirms `Sistema Compreensivo`, `classificação`, `Determinante`, `Sumário Estrutural`, and `Códigos Especiais` in Brazilian peer-reviewed use.
- [Localização e qualidade formal do Rorschach-SC no Brasil](https://www.scielo.br/j/pusf/a/kFHxFGKH3qx9gdVtyC6nqWS/), Psico-USF, 2010. Confirms `codificação`, `qualidade evolutiva`, `determinantes`, `qualidade formal`, `conteúdos`, and `códigos especiais`.
- [Indicadores do Rorschach no Sistema Compreensivo](https://www.scielo.br/j/pcp/a/QWGt9mkCyJpJ7X9sD3TZ6MS/?format=pdf&lang=pt), Psicologia: Ciência e Profissão, 2021. Provides Brazilian labels for `ativo`, `passivo`, `movimento passivo`, and Structural Summary variables.
- [Estudo normativo para o Sistema Compreensivo do Rorschach](https://pepsic.bvsalud.org/scielo.php?script=sci_arttext&pid=S1677-04712012000300005), Avaliação Psicológica / PePSIC, 2012. Brazilian peer-reviewed use of `Sistema Compreensivo`, administration and coding vocabulary.

`inquérito` for the Inquiry phase is confirmed by Brazilian peer-reviewed Comprehensive System literature. The recurring coding label is `classificação como ativo ou passivo`, while separate counts are described as `frequências de movimentos ativos e passivos` (distinct from the combined `a-p` code).

## Review Record

For each review batch, record the date, reviewer, source additions, terms changed, and unresolved terms in the review report. Do not put agent conversations or draft history into reader-facing README files or release notes.

### 2026-07-19 — independent editorial review (Claude Opus 4.8)

- Sources added: English (Holaday 1996; Exner 2003 manual), Japanese (金剛出版 record form and authorized translation; cross-checked with the 日本ロールシャッハ学会 record-form listing), Spanish (Psimática authorized manuals; Sendín), Brazilian Portuguese (PePSIC 2012 normative study).
- Confirmed against authoritative sources: `構造一覧表` (ja), `encuesta` (es Inquiry), `Sistema Comprehensivo` (es), `Sistema Compreensivo` and `inquérito` (pt-BR), and the English Comprehensive System terms.
- Corrected in the companion documents: pt-BR `frequências ativas-passivas` / `movimento ativo-passivo` → `frequências ativas e passivas` / `movimento ativo e passivo` (separate active and passive frequencies, distinct from the combined `a-p` code); en "Regression tests fix the CDI boundary" → "lock in" (avoids reading "fix" as repairing a defect).
- Unresolved: the Japanese prose noun phrase for active/passive quality (`能動・受動の性質` in the glossary vs `能動・受動の質` in the documents). Neither variant was matched to the authorized 金剛出版 translation in this review; it must be confirmed against the printed manual before the glossary and the affected groups can be marked `reviewed`.
- Open editorial item (not a terminology block): companion version-type labels are rendered inconsistently within a language (for example en "Minor patch" vs "Minor release"). The owner should choose whether companions mirror the Korean `패치` label or use semantic release/patch English before the pilot is finalized.

### 2026-07-21 — final terminology and release-label review

- Japanese active/passive terminology was settled as `運動の活動性（能動・受動）`, with `能動・受動の区別がない` used in natural explanatory prose, after checking Takase's peer-reviewed Japanese article.
- Spanish active/passive terminology was settled as `superíndice activo o pasivo` from the authorized coding-manual terminology. Brazilian Portuguese uses `classificação como ativo ou passivo`, supported by peer-reviewed descriptions of active and passive movement.
- Release-category labels were standardized separately for Korean, English, Japanese, neutral Spanish, and Brazilian Portuguese. The approved mapping is stored in `manifest.json` and enforced by the locale verifier.
- No recurring clinical terminology remains unresolved in the current seven-group pilot.
